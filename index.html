Let's Make Every Dog A Happy Dog

Loyal Rescue was established in 2008 and is a foster-based rescue with all-volunteer workers. We were established in Ontario, Canada and strive to help dogs find a home and also rehabilitate those who have been injured or have emotional harm. We take all dogs no matter the situation and we are determined to find all dogs a home. You can help us make every dog a happy dog!
